<!--REST-->
<b>Express Gratitude.</b> Gratitude is an attitude you can choose, even when things are difficult. Giving and receiving appreciation reminds us that we are not alone and belong to something bigger than ourselves.<br /><b>Try this:</b> <i>Make a list of people and things you are grateful for today. Add to the list over time. Express your thanks daily. Notice how it makes you feel.</i>
